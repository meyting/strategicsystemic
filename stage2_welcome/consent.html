Introduction

Thank you for participating in our study, which is conducted by the Johannes Gutenberg University of Mainz. The objective of this study is to collect information for research on economic decision-making.

Data

Before you start, we would like to ask you for your consent for participation. You may withdraw your consent at any time by closing this survey/tab. Once you have withdrawn your consent, your personal data will no longer be used. All your data is anonymous, so that your responses cannot be associated with your person. The University Mainz is committed to protecting your privacy.

If you have any questions about this study, desire additional information, or wish to withdraw your consent, please get in touch with the researcher via Prolific. If you have read this information and have decided to participate in this project, please understand that your participation is voluntary and that you have the right to discontinue participation at any time without penalty. In this case, you will not receive a participation fee. Your individual privacy will be maintained in all published and written data resulting from the study.

All data that you enter in this survey will be treated anonymously and will solely be used for academic research. Neither the researcher nor anyone else will be able to identify you from the answers that you give in this survey.
